# LID_mapping_falciparum_resistance

This repository holds the supplementary data accompanying the paper "Mapping the prevalence of molecular markers of Plasmodium falciparum artemisinin partial resistance in Africa: a systematic review and spatiotemporal modelling study", Wernsman Young & Meier-Scherling et al. (2026).

It contains the following files:

- `marker_prevalence_2026.03.17.xlsx` - an excel spreadsheet containing the raw data prevalence (numerator & denominator) at mutations covered in the paper (k13, crt 76, mdr1 86) for all unique surveys. Note that this is the exact same information as present in the STAVE object present in the [analysis repository]([ff](https://github.com/IDEELResearch/scrub/releases/tag/published)). The STAVE version is recommended for those working in R code, and this spreadsheet version for manual exploration.
- `review-reference-lists.xlsx` - details of inclusions and exclusions for the systematic review
- `bias_report.xlsx` - a risk of bias analysis of all studies identified in the systematic review
- `PRISMA Checklist.doc` - record of where all aspects of PRISMA requirements are met within the paper
